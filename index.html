First Up: DiFara

This pizza can be found in the quiet Brooklyn neighborhood known as Midwood. It's a popular spot due to its close proximity to several schools so you can expect to wait at least 20 minutes on its busiest days. And the quality of its pizza keeps the customers coming back for more. The pizza is filling but not heavy.
